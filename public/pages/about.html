About Us

The Broadneck Robotics Club, est. 2999, comprises a group of students at Broadneck High School who enjoy learning to Build, Code, and use other engineering principles to build competitive robots. We use the VEX V5 system to compete in VRC tournaments and events during the fall and winter season. We also work on other projects during the spring before resuming work on competitive bots in May.
